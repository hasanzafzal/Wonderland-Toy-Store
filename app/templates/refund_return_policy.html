Return Policy

30-Day Return Window

At Wonderland Toy Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Condition: Items must be in original, unused condition with all original packaging intact
  • Tags: All tags and labels must be attached and undamaged
  • Accessories: All original accessories, batteries, and manuals must be included
  • Time Frame: Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Receipt: Original receipt or proof of purchase is required

Items Eligible for Return

  • Unopened and sealed toy boxes
  • Defective or damaged items (unopened)
  • Wrong items received
  • Items that don't match the product description

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used items
  • Items missing original packaging or tags
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Custom or personalized items

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact Us: Email our support team at support_WTS@H&H.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. Receive Return Label: We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label via email
  3. Pack Your Item: Carefully pack your item in original packaging and include all original materials
  4. Ship Back: Use the provided return label to ship the item back to us
  5. Inspection: Once received, we'll inspect the item to verify return eligibility
  6. Refund/Exchange: Upon approval, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days

Refund Policy

Refund Amount

  • Full Refund: Original purchase price (excluding shipping fees)
  • Return Shipping: Free return shipping label provided for eligible returns
  • Original Shipping: Non-refundable (unless return is due to our error)

Refund Timeline

  • Inspection: 2-3 business days after receiving your return
  • Processing: 5-7 business days for refund to appear in your account
  • Bank Processing: Additional 2-3 business days depending on your financial institution

Partial Refunds

If an item is returned in used or damaged condition (but still accepted), a partial refund of 50-75% may be issued at our discretion.

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, we'll provide a replacement or full refund immediately:

  • Report the issue within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the defect or damage
  • We'll arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you
  • Free return shipping label will be provided

Exchanges

Want to exchange your item for a different product?

  • Request an exchange within 30 days of delivery
  • If the new item costs more, you'll need to pay the difference
  • If the new item costs less, we'll refund the difference
  • Free return shipping provided; standard shipping on replacement

Special Circumstances

Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, contact us immediately and we'll:

  • Send you the correct item right away
  • Provide a free return label for the wrong item
  • No questions asked - completely free to you

Item Doesn't Match Description

If the product doesn't match our description or photos:

  • Full refund or replacement available
  • Free return shipping provided
  • No restocking fees apply

Late Arrivals

If your order arrives after the expected delivery date:

  • You'll receive a 10% refund on your purchase
  • Or store credit for future purchases
  • Contact us for assistance - we'll make it right

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return an item I opened to check?

If you opened the packaging but the item is completely unused and in perfect condition, we may accept it. Contact our support team with photos for evaluation.

What if I'm outside the 30-day return window?

If you're slightly outside the 30-day window and the item is defective, we may still help. Contact our support team and we'll do our best to assist you.

How long does a refund take?

Once we receive and inspect your return, processing takes 5-7 business days. Your bank may take an additional 2-3 business days to show the refund.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

We provide free return shipping labels for all eligible returns. For items outside our return policy, you're responsible for return shipping costs.

Can I return items purchased during a sale?

Yes! Sale items are returnable under the same policy. Refunds are based on the sale price paid, not the original retail price.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement immediately at no cost to you, with prepaid return shipping for the damaged item.
